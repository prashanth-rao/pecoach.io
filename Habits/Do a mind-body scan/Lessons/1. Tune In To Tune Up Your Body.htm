Paying attention pays off.

If you're not used to paying attention to your body in a focused way, this might feel weird.

Weird is OK.

You might find yourself super-focused on odd sensations, like a single hair wafting in the breeze from your air conditioner, or wondering whether your right baby toenail has always felt like that.

Look for times in your day when you know you'll be in a relatively more private and quiet space.

Some options include:

  • In your car before you drive somewhere, or just after you arrive
  • Just before falling asleep, or just after waking up
  • Just after a workout
  • In the bathroom
