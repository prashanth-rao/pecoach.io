Coach's Notes: How to use the mind-body scan for self-awareness during movement.

Take a mind-body scan to the gym.

Practice more mindful training

The next time you're at the gym, remember to pause. Take a few moments, stare off into space like you're having a deep thought, and do a quick mind-body scan.

Some questions to ask yourself

Do you notice any points of tightness or restriction in your body? Any parts that felt especially strong or mobile?

What do you feel as you do a particular exercise? Are you recruiting the right muscles? Can you perceive what your body is doing?

Can you make small physical adjustments, if necessary? Reading your body cues, could you improve your exercise form?

Do you notice any thoughts you had, or assumptions, about the workout? How did those thoughts and assumptions evolve during the workout?

Did anything surprise you? Did anything change?

What feels good about this workout? (Pause to kiss your mighty biceps, as needed.)
