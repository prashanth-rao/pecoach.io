Ways To Do A Mind-Body Scan

Are you hearing thoughts, emotions, or physical sensations?

Observe your body cues

Before each meal, take 30-60 seconds to do a quick "mind-body scan".

During the meal, pause now and again to do the scan again. Put your fork down and take a moment to check in.

  • What do you feel right now, really?
  • How is that separate from a narration?
  • Is what you're feeling different from what you're telling yourself you feel?

For now, try to notice and name whatever you feel, and don't worry about what to do with those feelings or try to change them.

Some questions you could ask yourself are:

  • If I feel any anxiety or stress, how does that show up in my body and movement? For instance, am I fidgeting? Furrowing my brow? Etc.
  • If I feel happiness or joy, where do I feel it in my body?
  • If I feel energetic, where do I feel it in my body?
  • If I feel hungry (or full), where do I feel it in my body?

And listen.
