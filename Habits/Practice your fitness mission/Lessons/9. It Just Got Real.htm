It Just Got Real

If you were forced to live your dreams… how would you do it?

Coach's Notes: Making unfulfilled desires and dreams slightly more real (since many clients' poor habits reflect overall life dissatisfaction or boredom).

How to be awesome.

Would you like to have an adventure? Be a hero? Do something exciting and meaningful?

How do you learn to do that? There are not many How To Be Awesome instruction manuals.

How to be awesome

Find someone who is doing the "thing" that you want to be doing in life.

Someone who is—right now—living the dream.

Learn about how that person does what they do.

Spend some time with them, if you can. Interview them. Exchange an email or two. Or just simply observe their process.

De-mystify the hero.

Humanize the person who seems to be "better" than you, because they're successful at something you've always wanted to try or do. Or be.

Instead, learn how they did what they did.

It's probably not magic.

How could you imitate their process or behaviors? Even just a little bit?

How could you get "there" from "here"?

How could you make it happen?

Once you gather the information, ask yourself: If I absolutely had to do this thing... how could I do it?

If you had no choice but to follow your dream... how would you make it happen?

What do your role models say?

How could you imitate what they do?

How are they "average people" just like you… who've simply made different choices?

How could you help yourself make those choices in the future?

Enjoy the excitement. Anything is possible.

Does thinking about it make your heart jump? Do you feel a bit woozy or lightheaded? Kinda want to barf a little, but in a good way?

Are you wearing a dumb grin on your face without even realizing it?

A world of adventures lie before you. Perhaps closer than you imagined.

Think about how your fitness mission aligns with your dreams.

Can you bring your mission and dreams together? If so, how?
