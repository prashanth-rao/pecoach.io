What Would You Do?

Oh, the adventures you could have!

Coach's Notes: Imagine a new Destination Postcard, updating the possibilities with your new healthier / fitter self.

Oh, the adventures you'll have ...

Imagine that you have seamlessly, fully integrated healthy eating and living into your life.

You're easily hitting basic healthy eating practices, every day without even thinking about it, loving your vegetables, walking the dog every day, and sleeping like a tranquilized log.

What would you do with the rest of the time?

If you weren't being such an avid fitness hobbyist, what else would you do that was meaningful? Fun? Interesting? Relevant?

What's keeping you from doing those things?

Change course

While it's perfectly fine to want a lean and healthy body for life, you can't lose fat forever. At some point, you have to change focus.

Think about what's next for you

Focus on what you might work towards doing in the next 3 to 6 months. Can you start to incorporate that vision as a goal?

  • What excites you?
  • What's fun? What makes you go "wheee!"?
  • What scares or challenges you physically?
  • What makes you feel engaged in life and the world/people around you?
  • What makes you feel connected to and proud of your own body?
  • What do you think is valuable and important in life?
  • What makes you feel comfortable and confident in your own skin?
  • What makes you feel strong, fast, powerful, skilled, capable—or just able to handle life?

There's no endpoint to a fitness journey.
