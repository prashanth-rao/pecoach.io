Dear Newbie

Help a beginner with some encouragement and advice.

You've made some changes.

Think about your experience in making deliberate changes in your life. What insights have you gained? About yourself, nutrition, exercise, or living the good life?

And think about how this experience and insight gives you empathy—the ability to connect based on shared feelings. To recognize and—to some degree—"get" other people's struggles and victories.

Where did you come from?

Do you remember how you used to be? What's different now than then? What are the changes that have most defined "before" and "after"? Try to remember the full picture of "before"...

  • What were you doing?
  • What were you thinking?
  • What did you feel?
  • How did your body look? Perform? Feel?

Once you have a clear, rich image, put someone else in your place.

Imagine yourself as another person. A newbie, just like you, starting this coaching program.

What would you say?

Imagine sitting down with that newbie. You're the older, wiser athlete. You understand how they feel and think.

  • What advice do you give them?
  • What would you say to help them?
  • What would you share of your own experience?

Write down in the space below how you could help them on the start of their own coaching journey.
