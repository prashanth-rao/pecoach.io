Refresh Your Space

Refresh your space to stay on track.

We're strongly influenced by our environment.

Subtle (or not-so-subtle) cues from our surroundings can change our behavior, without us being aware of it. To be your own best buddy...

Keep your immediate environment as positive and productive as possible.

Don't keep stuff around that harms you. Aim to make everything around you help you meet your goals.

A healthy environment checklist

Your kitchen

  • Have you eliminated junk food?
  • Have you eliminated any other foods that are "trigger foods" for you?
  • What about drinks?
  • Is your kitchen well-stocked with a variety of healthy foods?
  • Are those healthy foods easy to get to and prepare?

    • The rest of your house

  • Have you eliminated other "triggers" from the rest of the house? (e.g., asking people to smoke outside if you're trying to quit smoking)
  • Do you have other things in your house that help you be healthy? (e.g., a garden, a few dumbbells)
  • Is your bedroom a groovy, relaxing sleep sanctuary?

    • Your daily routine

  • Do you plan and prepare ways to help yourself do your practices, lessons, and workouts?
  • What about rest, relaxation, and recovery activities?
  • Do you anticipate and strategize around obstacles?
  • Do you expect the unexpected? (e.g., running late, forgetting lunch)
  • Can you re-adjust your healthy practices "on the fly"? (e.g., ordering in restaurants, social events)
  • Do you have creative solutions to common problems? (e.g., cycling instead of driving to work; getting a healthy meal delivery service for times when you're busy)
  • Do you have and stick to a sleep ritual? What about your weekly food prep ritual?
  • Do you regularly check in with motivating and inspiring images, videos, role models, reminders, or other cues?
  • Does your daily routine and schedule clearly reflect your top priorities?

    • Your household and social circle

  • Have you shared your health and wellness goals, needs, and expectations with the people you live with?
  • What about your friends and acquaintances?
  • Have you negotiated ways to ensure that you can successfully do your lessons, practices, and workouts? (e.g., shuffling child care, blocking off "workout time")
  • Have you found like-minded people and built a support network—whether that's at home, at your gym, at your workplace, etc.?
  • Do most of your family members and friends support you, inspire you, and help you move towards your goals?
  • Have you cut down on hanging out with negative people or people who hinder your progress?
