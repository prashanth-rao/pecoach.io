Be your own coach

Be the author of your own story.

If you were your own coaching client, what would you suggest?

1.   What does my client (i.e., me) need, right now, to keep moving forward?

2.   What's one very small task that I know my client can realistically do, today and every day for the next two weeks?

Keep it small

You may have a lot you want to change or improve.

Just pick one thing.

And keep it small. Lasting change happens only in small steps.

One step at a time, one practice at a time.

These steps have to be as small as possible, so that the client (that's you) can do them every day, and feel successful.

Keep it simple

No grand plans. No elaborate schemes.

This should to be something that your client (aka you) can realistically, for sure, do every day.

So keep it simple.

A few coaching tips

1.   Once you come up with the task, make it even smaller.

You should feel ready, willing, and able to do it, with full confidence.

2.   Do the practice you gave yourself.

Stick with it for 2 weeks. If you notice any trouble doing the task, just make a note of it.

3.   Help yourself remember.

Now you're the coach to yourself. What does your client need in order to remember what to do?

Whatever that is, help your client do their assigned task for today.
